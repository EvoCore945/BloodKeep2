"My name... has long since faded from memory. But they once called me the Crimson Watcher. I was a sentinel of the old order, sworn to guard the rift between worlds—until we failed."
"Bloodkeep was our last stronghold, buried beneath cursed stone and silence. When the demons broke through, it became a tomb of screaming iron, sealed with three shards torn from the very soul of the keep."
"Now, centuries later, echoes stir once more. Whispers claw at the veil. I feel the pull... something awakens in the dark. I must return—not for redemption, but to end what should have never been allowed to breathe."
"The path is carved in blood. And I will walk it alone."
